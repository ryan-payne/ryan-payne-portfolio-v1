Why I Want to Work at Basecamp

First, I use and love Basecamp’s products. I started with Basecamp Classic way back in 2008 and use Basecamp Personal for freelance projects today. I admire the product decisions and progress I’ve experienced firsthand.

I've read or re-read Rework, Remote, It Doesn't Have to Be Crazy at Work, and Shape Up. I AM ON BOARD. Your philosophies closely align with how I've come to view design, product development, and work life.

I've learned so much over the years from intelligent and forward-thinking Basecamp designers, programmers, support staff, and your excellent podcast. Shout out to Wailin and Shaun.

I'll add my ability to reframe complex problems and solve them with simple, intuitive interfaces while continuing to learn from some of the best in the business.

Finally, your benefits are incredible. It's clear that you value and trust your employees – I want some of that.
